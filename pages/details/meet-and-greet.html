Meet and Greet

Date: June 12th, 2015

Time: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Where: 412 Louisana Street Little Rock, AR 72201

Description: Classmates will meet and greet former classmates while enjoying finger foods, drinks, and taking pictures. Each classmate will be given their gift bags and other material needed for the remainder of the weekend. Also, each classmate will be given 2 tickets to purchase 2 alcoholic or non-alcoholic beverages at the bar which is included in their overall dues. Any drinks after the use of the tickets the classmates will be responsible to pay themselves. Once our time spot at Club Sway has expired, classmates can either stay at the Club that opens at 10pm or can go hang out in the River Market, which is only blocks away from the Club Sway.

* Details are subject to change based on the amount of registrants
